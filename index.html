The Muse

Redefining
Heritage

Priya Solanki is a contemporary high-fashion model redefining Indian luxury. Blending the regal symmetry of her Gujarati heritage with modern avant-garde sensibilities, she brings a commanding presence to runway and print alike.

Hailing from Anand, Gujarat, Priya represents the fluid transition between classic cultural grandeur and modern minimalism, working with heritage couture houses and editorial campaigns.
